# Theme Visual Distinctions

**Purpose:** Document what makes each theme visually unique

---

## Quick Summary

You were right - Modern and Classic initially only differed in colors. I've now added more visual distinction:

| Theme | Typography | Colors | Spacing | Feel |
|-------|-----------|--------|---------|------|
| **Modern Minimal** | Moderate scale (1.25 ratio) | Neutral blue/gray | Standard 8px grid | Clean, professional, safe |
| **Classic Elegant** | Larger scale (1.333 ratio) | Warm cream/terracotta | More generous spacing | Sophisticated, established, traditional |
| **Bold Editorial** | Moderate scale + Sans fonts | Dark bg/hot pink | Standard spacing | Dramatic, fashion-forward, contemporary |

---

## Modern Minimal (Default)

**Typography:**
- Scale: 1.25 ratio (48px → 38px → 30px → 24px...)
- Fonts: Playfair Display (headings) + Inter (body)
- Weight: Standard hierarchy

**Colors:**
- Background: Pure white
- Text: Near black
- Accent: Vibrant blue (hsl 220, 90%, 56%)

**Spacing:**
- Standard 8px grid (24px, 32px, 48px, 64px sections)

**Personality:**
- Neutral, professional, lets work shine
- Similar to contemporary theatre/film portfolios
- Safe choice for broad appeal

**Best for:** Sarah (contemporary theatre), general professional use

---

## Classic Elegant

**Typography:**
- Scale: 1.333 ratio (67px → 50px → 37px → 28px...) - **20-30% larger headlines**
- Fonts: Playfair Display (headings) + Inter (body) - **Same as Modern**
- Body: 18px instead of 16px - **More luxurious reading experience**
- Line-height: Tighter (1.05 display, 1.15 headings) - **More dramatic**

**Colors:**
- Background: Warm cream (hsl 40, 20%, 98%)
- Text: Dark warm brown (hsl 30, 20%, 15%)
- Accent: Rich terracotta (hsl 25, 60%, 45%)

**Spacing:**
- More generous: 32px, 48px, 64px, 96px sections (**25-50% more space**)

**Personality:**
- Established, sophisticated, gravitas
- Traditional editorial aesthetic
- Signals experience and prestige

**Best for:** Emma (20+ years experience), established professionals

---

## Bold Editorial

**Typography:**
- Scale: 1.25 ratio (same as Modern) - **Moderate hierarchy**
- Fonts: **Sora (headings) + Geist Sans (body)** - **Completely different fonts**
- Weight: Same as Modern

**Colors:**
- Background: Near black (hsl 0, 0%, 5%)
- Text: Near white (hsl 0, 0%, 98%)
- Accent: Hot pink (hsl 340, 85%, 55%) - **Statement color**

**Spacing:**
- Standard 8px grid (same as Modern)

**Personality:**
- Dramatic, bold, fashion-forward
- High contrast for impact
- Contemporary, edgy, confident

**Best for:** Marcus (fashion designer), avant-garde work, making a statement

## How to Compare in Browser

**Theme switcher is now in the header (top-right corner)**

1. **Start on Modern Minimal** (default)
   - Notice the moderate headlines, blue accent, neutral feel

2. **Switch to Classic Elegant**
   - Watch headlines get MUCH LARGER (67px vs 48px display)
   - Notice more breathing room (generous spacing)
   - See warm cream background vs pure white
   - Feel the shift to traditional/editorial aesthetic

3. **Switch to Bold Editorial**
   - FONTS CHANGE: Sora/Geist Sans instead of Playfair/Inter
   - Background flips to dark
   - Hot pink accent vs blue or terracotta
   - Dramatic contrast for fashion/contemporary feel

## What to Look For

**Typography Scale:**
- Classic has noticeably larger headlines - does this feel more prestigious?
- Modern/Bold use moderate scale - does this feel more contemporary?

**Font Pairings:**
- Modern/Classic: Playfair Display (serif) headings - traditional, elegant
- Bold: Sora (sans-serif) headings - modern, geometric, fashion-forward

**Spacing:**
- Classic: More white space - does this feel premium or wasteful?
- Modern/Bold: Standard spacing - efficient or cramped?

**Color Psychology:**
- Modern: Blue = trust, professional, safe
- Classic: Terracotta/cream = warm, established, sophisticated
- Bold: Pink/black = edgy, fashion, contemporary

**Overall:**
- Which theme feels most appropriate for costume designers?
- Do they feel distinct enough to offer real choice?
- Is any theme too conservative? Too aggressive?
